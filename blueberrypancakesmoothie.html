Blueberry Pancake Smoothie

Recipe Details

Prep: 5 mins

Cook: 5 mins

Total: 10 mins

Serving: 1 serving

Yield: 1 blueberry pancake smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 1 banana
  • 1 pitted date
  • 2 tbsp rolled oats
  • 1 tbsp cashew butter
  • 1 tbsp protein powder
  • 1 tsp Childlife Essentials Vitamin C

Instructions

  1. Combine 1 cup almond milk, 1/2 cup frozen blueberries, 1 banana, 1 pitted date, 2 tbsp rolled oats, 1 tbsp cashew butter, 1 tbsp protein powder, and 1 tsp Childlife Essentials Vitamin C.
  2. Blend until creamy and smooth.
  3. Enjoy!
