Rice Paper Rolls

Prep, Cook, Total, Servings, and Yield

Prep: 10 mins

Cook: 10 mins

Total: 20 mins

Servings: 12 rolls

Yield: 12 Rice paper rolls

Ingredients

  • Tofu/Tempeh
  • 250 g firm tofu or tempeh
  • 3 Tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • Rice Paper Rolls
  • 2 zucchini or zucchini noodles 300 grams
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 cucumber
  • ¼ head red cabbage
  • 1 handful fresh herbs
  • vermicelli (optional)
  • 12 rice paper (12 if going single layer or 24 if doubling up)

Instructions

  1. In a large flat-bottomed dish, whisk together the soy sauce, brown sugar, and sesame oil.
  2. Slice your firm tofu into slices. Lie the tofu slices in the marinade.
  3. Flip the tofu over so that both sides are coated, and leave to rest.
  4. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F).
  5. Bake in the hot oven for 30 minutes, turning once during cooking.
  6. Remove from the oven and allow to cool.
  7. Prepare the vegetables by slicing them into long thin batons or using a food processor.
  8. Fill a large bowl with room temperature water. Soak the rice paper until softened.
  9. Lay the softened rice paper smooth side down on your work surface.
  10. Lay the vegetables, then tofu, and then herbs on the rice paper.
  11. Fold the sides of the rice paper towards the center, then roll tightly.
  12. Cover the rolls with a damp cloth to prevent drying out.
