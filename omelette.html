Easy Kids Omelette

Prep, Cook, Total, Serving, and Yield

Prep: 5 mins

Cook: 5 mins

Total: 10 mins

Serving: 1 serving

Yield: 1 omelet

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray, or butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • Meat, or vegetables (such as sliced sausage, onions, bell peppers, spinach, etc.), optional
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Spray a 10- or 12-inch non-stick or cast iron skillet with a thin coating of cooking spray and heat over medium heat.
  3. Beat eggs, water, salt, and pepper until light and fluffy.
  4. When skillet is hot (but not smoking), pour egg mixture in.
  5. As omelet starts to set, lift an edge of the omelet with a clean spatula and tilt pan so that the runny, unset portion of omelet can run underneath and start to set. Repeat on the opposite side.
  6. Add the other ingredients as desired.
  7. When omelet is just about completely set, sprinkle the cheese over half of the omelet and fold. Serve immediately.
