Pasta Salad

Prep, Cook, Total, Servings, and Yield

Prep: 10 mins

Cook: 10 mins

Total: 20 mins

Servings: 10 servings

Yield: Pasta salad mixture

Ingredients

  • 500 g spiral pasta
  • Â¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 270 g sun dried tomatoes
  • 400 g corn kernels
  • 75 g salami
  • 3 handfuls baby spinach leaves
  • 150 g feta

Instructions

  1. Cook your pasta as per the directions on the packet. Be sure not to overcook your pasta; you want it firm. Cool the pasta by running it under cold water, and allow it to drain well.
  2. Drain the corn kernels, slice the salami into strips, and crumble the feta.
  3. Put your mayonnaise in a small mixing bowl. Drain the oil from the jar of sun-dried tomatoes into the same bowl. Whisk to combine; this is the dressing for the pasta salad.
  4. Stir the dressing through your cooked, cooled pasta so that all the pasta has a light coating. Taste your pasta and season with salt and pepper as needed.
  5. Add the remaining ingredients and toss the salad so they are evenly distributed. Refrigerate until serving.
