So, Who Am I?

Hi there! I’m Mari Morales, a 27-year-old mother of three wonderful children, living in the beautiful Colorado Springs. Family is the heartbeat of my life, and I’ve always cherished the moments we share in the kitchen. To me, cooking isn’t just about preparing meals—it’s about creating memories and nurturing those we love.

In 2011, I embarked on a culinary journey, inspired by the desire to be more involved in my children's diet and to bring healthy, tasty food into our home. What started as a personal mission to ensure my family enjoyed nutritious meals has blossomed into a passion that I’m excited to share with you. Over the years, I’ve discovered that cooking at home can be a delightful adventure, filled with the joy of experimenting with new flavors and techniques, all while keeping our meals wholesome and enjoyable.

This website is more than just a collection of recipes—it’s a celebration of the simple, joyful moments that happen when families come together in the kitchen. Whether you’re searching for quick and easy weeknight dinners, fun and creative snacks that kids will love, or a bit of culinary adventure to spice up your routine, I hope you find something here that resonates with you.

My goal is to inspire and support families in making cooking a fun and enriching experience. Let’s turn meal preparation into a cherished activity and make lasting memories through the art of cooking. I’m thrilled to have you join me on this journey—let’s make every meal a joyful celebration!
